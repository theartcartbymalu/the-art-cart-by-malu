I hold a BTech degree in Computer Science & Engineering and began my professional journey as a Software Tester. Currently, I work in an engineering company near Chalakudy.

Why I started this:

I never planned to start this as a business. In May 2023, I designed a baby details photo frame for my own child purely out of love and happiness. I shared it on my WhatsApp status, and to my surprise, friends began asking me to create similar frames.

I initially said no, but their encouragement made me realize that this small moment of happiness could mean something special to others. I started with photo frames and polaroids, and over time, as trends changed, my creativity naturally evolved into customized gift hampers for all occasions.
